Safety & Preparation

At Brush & Broom LLP, your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. We follow strict protocols and best practices to ensure every project is done safely, efficiently, and with minimal disruption.

Our Safety Standards

  • All our team members are trained and certified in workplace safety.
  • We use high-quality, non-toxic, and environmentally friendly materials whenever possible.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is worn by all staff during work.
  • Equipment is regularly inspected and maintained for safe operation.
  • We follow all local regulations and industry standards.

Preparation Tips for Clients

  • Please clear the work areas of personal belongings to protect your items and allow us easy access.
  • For painting jobs, move fragile furniture away from walls or floors, or cover them securely.
  • Ensure pets and children are kept safely away from work zones during service.
  • If power washing near windows, doors, or vents, please close and secure them to avoid water damage.
  • We recommend communicating any special needs or concerns before work begins.

COVID-19 Precautions

  • We maintain strict hygiene standards, including sanitizing tools and hands regularly.
  • We practice social distancing and wear masks when inside homes or businesses as requested.
  • If you have any health concerns, please inform us ahead of time so we can accommodate safely.

Thank you for trusting Brush & Broom LLP. We’re committed to delivering quality results with your safety and comfort in mind.
