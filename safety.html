Our Safety & Prep Work Promise

At Brush & Broom LLP, safety isn’t just a checklist — it’s the core of how we work. We take pride in protecting your home, your surfaces, and our crew at every stage of the job.

We prep every surface properly — from masking, drop cloths, sanding, and caulking to priming, ventilation, and proper curing. We wear PPE, maintain jobsite cleanliness, and prioritize communication.

Every job is done like we’re doing it for our own families. That means zero shortcuts, complete respect for your property, and a result you can trust.
