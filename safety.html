Our Safety & Prep Work Promise

At Brush & Broom LLP, safety isn't just a checklist — it's how we start every job. We care deeply about protecting your home, our workers, and the quality of your space from start to finish.

From careful masking, surface prep, sanding, and pressure testing to drop cloths, ventilation, and PPE, we approach every task with professionalism and caution. We clean as we go, communicate with you often, and leave your space looking better than ever.

Every project is treated as if it’s our own home — with safety, durability, and long-term quality always top of mind.
