What we clean

A full reset from kitchen to baseboards

Move-out and move-in cleans go deeper than our standard visit. We’re aiming for “inspection-ready” and “photo-ready,” not just “picked up.”

Kitchen

  • Counters, sinks, and backsplashes cleaned and sanitized.
  • Outside of cabinets, drawers, and appliances wiped.
  • Inside fridge, oven, and microwave available on request.
  • Floors swept and mopped, baseboards wiped.

Bathrooms

  • Toilets, tubs/showers, and sinks scrubbed and sanitized.
  • Mirrors, fixtures, and glass polished.
  • Cabinet fronts wiped; inside on request.
  • Floors cleaned, corners and grout lines addressed as needed.

Living areas & bedrooms

  • Dusting of reachable surfaces, ledges, and window sills.
  • Doors, door frames, light switches, and handles wiped.
  • Baseboards wiped where accessible.
  • Floors vacuumed and/or mopped throughout.
