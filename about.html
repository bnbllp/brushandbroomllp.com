About Brush & Broom LLP

Family-owned, community-driven cleaning and painting services across Bellevue, Kirkland, Redmond, and Eastside King County.

Our Story

Brush & Broom LLP started with a simple idea: life gets messy, and everyone deserves a clean, welcoming space without stress. Founded by a husband-and-wife team, we bring small-town hospitality, professionalism, and a personal touch to every home and office we serve.

We show up as the same friendly faces, respect your schedule, and leave your space sparkling. From eco-friendly cleaning to detailed painting services, our goal is to make your home feel cared for and comfortable.

Our Mission

  • Honesty, integrity, and quality workmanship
  • Creating opportunities for young or inexperienced workers
  • Supporting second-chance employees
  • Providing discounted or free services to seniors, disabled individuals, or those facing hardship

We believe success isn’t just about growth—it’s about lifting others up along the way.
