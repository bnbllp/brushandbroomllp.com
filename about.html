About Us

We're Brush & Broom LLP — a family-run painting and cleaning company based in Walla Walla, WA. With a deep love for transforming spaces, we bring energy, humor, and professionalism to every job.

We started with a passion for craftsmanship and a mission to bring integrity, beauty, and attention to detail into homes and businesses throughout the region. Whether it's fresh paint, sparkling clean move-outs, power washing, siding, or flooring — we do it all with pride.

Our team is hardworking, family-oriented, and recovery-strong. We believe in second chances, great work, and leaving every space better than we found it.
