About Brush & Broom LLP

At Brush & Broom LLP, we take pride in providing dependable, high-quality home improvement and cleaning services throughout Kirkland, Bellevue, and surrounding areas. What began as a small local business in Eastern Washington has grown into a trusted partner for homeowners and property managers across King County.

We specialize in detailed cleaning, expert interior and exterior painting, flooring installation and restoration, bathroom and kitchen remodeling, and professional handyman and maintenance work. Every project is handled with care, precision, and a commitment to exceptional results.

Our Core Values

✅ Integrity: We believe in transparent pricing and honest communication.

✅ Quality: From paint prep to clean finishes, every detail matters.

✅ Reliability: We show up on time and get the job done right.

✅ Community: We’re proud to serve local families, seniors, and small businesses in our area.
