Who We Are

A husband-and-wife team with a small-town work ethic.

We started Brush & Broom LLP because we saw a gap in the Seattle home services market. People were tired of being treated like a number by large franchises. They wanted someone they could trust in their home—someone who actually cared about the finished result.

Our background is rooted in hard work and a commitment to our community. We bring that same dedication to every home we clean and every wall we paint.
