About Brush & Broom LLP

Family-owned, community-driven cleaning and painting services across Bellevue, Kirkland, Redmond, and Eastside King County.

Our Story

Brush & Broom LLP started with a simple idea: life gets messy, and everyone deserves a clean, welcoming space without stress. Founded by a husband-and-wife duo, we bring small-town hospitality, professionalism, and a personal touch to every home and office we serve.

We’re not just another cleaning company. We show up as the same friendly faces, respect your schedule, and leave your space sparkling. From eco-friendly cleaning to upcoming painting services, our goal is to make your home feel cared for and comfortable.

Our Mission

We exist to provide high-quality cleaning and painting services while giving back to our community. We prioritize:

  • Honesty, integrity, and quality workmanship
  • Creating opportunities for young or inexperienced workers
  • Supporting second-chance employees
  • Providing discounted or free services to seniors, disabled individuals, or those facing hardship

We believe success isn’t just about growth—it’s about lifting others up along the way.
