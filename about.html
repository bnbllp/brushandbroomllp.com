About Brush & Broom LLP

Family-owned, community-first cleaning and painting services proudly based in Seattle and serving Kirkland and the Eastside.

Our Story

Brush & Broom LLP was built on a simple belief: people deserve honest work, fair pricing, and a home that feels cared for — not rushed through.

Founded by a husband-and-wife team, we combine professionalism with small-town values. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and treat every space with respect. Whether it’s detailed cleaning or precision painting, our goal is always the same — leave your home better than we found it.

What We Stand For

  • High standards, no shortcuts, and work we’re proud to put our name on
  • Honest communication and transparent pricing
  • Creating opportunities for young, inexperienced, or second-chance workers
  • Giving back through discounted or donated services for seniors and individuals facing hardship

Growth matters — but doing right by people matters more.
