FAQs & Cleaning Tips

How often should I schedule a cleaning?

It depends on your lifestyle! Many Eastside families prefer biweekly cleaning for regular maintenance, while deep cleaning or move-out services are scheduled as needed.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Nope! We can coordinate key pickup or secure access. Most clients choose to go about their day while we handle the work.

Are your cleaning products safe?

Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic products safe for kids, pets, and the environment, while still delivering sparkling results.

Can you handle special requests?

Absolutely. Just let us know if you have delicate surfaces, pet areas, or particular focus areas—we tailor every cleaning to your needs.

Tips for keeping your home cleaner longer:

  • Wipe kitchen counters daily to prevent buildup.
  • Use entryway mats to reduce tracked-in dirt.
  • Do small spot-cleaning tasks regularly to avoid heavy deep cleans.
  • Declutter surfaces for easier, faster cleaning sessions.
