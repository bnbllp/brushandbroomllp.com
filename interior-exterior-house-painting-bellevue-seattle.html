Our painting process

A simple, clear path from “we should repaint” to “I love this”

We’re big believers in communication and prep. Most paint jobs fail before the first coat goes on—not on our watch.

1
Walk-through & quote

We visit your home, walk each area with you, talk colors and goals, and send a clear written estimate with everything spelled out.

2
Colors & scheduling

We confirm your colors, finishes, and timing so you know what’s happening when—and how long we’ll need in your space.

3
Prep & protection

We protect floors and furniture, repair minor wall issues, sand and caulk as needed, and make sure surfaces are ready for paint.

4
Painting & cleanup

We apply quality paint with care, keep you updated as we go, and clean up thoroughly so you’re not left with a mess.
